Progressive web apps (PWA):

Need to develop a PWA? ASTech team has an impressive experience in PWA development and will be glad to assist you with your projects. Combine the best of web and mobile apps and let user avoid the unnecessary downloading and installation stages. The main advantage of PWA is the native-app user experience available in the browser. It also reduces the time and money spent on development as the same code runs across all the platforms and devices. Single-page apps:

Loading times are significantly shorter, which allows for improved navigation and therefore a smoother user experience. There is much less stress on the server since it no longer has to load a full page for each request.

What a single page app developed by a competent team can do for you, is boost your business operation.

  • According to recent studies, responsive and fast-loading pages are much more attractive for users.
  • They are an ideal tool for managing the display of dynamic content.
  • Unlike traditional web applications, they can work offline.
  • They allow modern interfaces to be created from legacy systems.
  • They are generally easy to upgrade.
  • And subsidiary bonus: a SPA is neither more nor less than a website designed as a mobile application, it can be used both from a smartphone, but also from a desktop!
